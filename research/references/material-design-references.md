## Key Characteristics to Identify in References:

### ✅ Authentic Material Design Markers:
- **8dp Grid System** - Everything aligns perfectly
- **Realistic Shadows** - Ambient + directional lighting
- **Roboto Typography** - Specific weights and sizes
- **Bold Colors** - Vibrant, saturated (not muted)
- **Elevation Hierarchy** - Clear 0dp to 24dp progression
- **4dp Corner Radius** - Cards and buttons
- **Material Motion** - Smooth, purposeful animations
- **FAB Placement** - Bottom-right, primary color, 6dp elevation

### ❌ Generic/Non-Material Elements to Avoid:
- Generic CSS shadows
- Random corner radiuses
- Muted or pastel colors
- Arbitrary spacing
- Multiple FABs per screen
- Non-Roboto fonts
- Flat design without elevation
